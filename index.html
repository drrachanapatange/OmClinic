The Holistic nature of homeopathy means each person is treated as a unique individual and their body, mind, spirit and emotions are all considered in the management and prevention of disease. A Homeopathy doctor will be interested in a patient as an individual and the unique way in which symptoms affect the patient.

Medicines are prepared from various sources that include plants, minerals, animal parts and secretions, human body secretions and other natural sources.

Homeopathy is a unique system of healing and has no adverse effect to the body, as it has no adverse and unwanted side effects. It is the best and safest mode of treatment for infants, children and also pregnant women.
